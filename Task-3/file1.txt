The Sweet Theft: 12 Tonnes of Missing KitKats
In a heist that sounds more like a Willy Wonka plot than a police report, Nestlé recently announced that 12 tonnes of KitKat bars were stolen in a daring "sweet" robbery. The massive shipment vanished during transport, leaving authorities puzzled and fans of the crispy chocolate wafer in a state of mild chocolate-deprivation anxiety. While high-value thefts usually involve electronics or jewelry, this "KitKat Caper" has proven that in 2026, sugar might just be the new gold.
